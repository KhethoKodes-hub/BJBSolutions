BJB Solutions is a dynamic, multi-faceted company based in Kathu, Northern Cape. Founded in 2024, the company is dedicated to offering a diverse range of services, including training programs, construction, commercial cleaning, gardening services, and the supply of quality goods.

Our mission is to deliver exceptional service with professionalism and integrity — contributing to the growth and development of our community and beyond.
